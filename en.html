Privacy Policy

Nailva — Nail Salon Manager · com.wowpia.nailshopmanager · Last updated: 2026-06-24 · Effective: 2026-06-24
Summary. Nailva stores customer data locally on your device by default. If you opt into Cloud Backup or use OAuth login, certain data is transmitted to our servers. Anonymous diagnostics, crash logs, and an advertising ID are collected to operate, improve, and monetize the app.

1. Operator

Operator: Info-Gate (wowpia)
Contact email: startline2011@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect and Share

The table below corresponds to the Google Play Data Safety declaration for this app.

2.1 Stored Only on Your Device (Not Collected)

The following data is entered by you and stored in a local SQLite database on your device. It is not transmitted to our servers unless you enable Cloud Backup (Section 2.2).

  • Customer names, phone numbers, notes
  • Service records and photos
  • Booking information
  • Revenue and statistics
  • App settings (language, theme)

2.2 Cloud Backup (Optional, Opt-In)

If you sign in via Settings → Cloud Backup using Google or Kakao OAuth, the following data is transmitted to and stored on our Nailoop backup server:

  • Email address, nickname, social account identifier (Google ID / Kakao ID)
  • Server-issued user ID and authentication tokens
  • Customer names, phone numbers, notes (your customer records)
  • Service records and photos
  • Booking information

You can stop the transmission at any time by signing out of Cloud Backup. To delete the data already on the server, see Section 5.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • App functionality: managing customers, bookings, photos, and revenue.
  • Account management: identifying you when using Cloud Backup or OAuth login.
  • Analytics: measuring feature usage to improve the app (PostHog, Firebase Analytics).
  • Diagnostics & crash reporting: detecting and fixing bugs (Crashlytics, Sentry).
  • Advertising: showing ads to support the free tier (AdMob).

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. The only third party that uses your data for its own purposes is Google (AdMob), which uses your advertising ID to serve ads. All other third-party services (Firebase, PostHog, Sentry) act as data processors under a data processing agreement and process data only on our behalf.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: stored until you delete it in-app or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud Backup server data: retained until you request deletion via email. See the Account Deletion Guide.
  • Sentry crash logs: auto-expire after 90 days.
  • PostHog analytics: auto-expire after 7 days.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: retention per Google's policy.
  • Anonymized aggregate statistics: may be retained indefinitely in non-identifiable form.

Account deletion process: https://info-gate.github.io/nailva/delete-account/

6. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS / TLS.
  • Local data is stored in the app's private sandbox on your device.
  • Server data is access-controlled and stored on infrastructure operated by Info-Gate.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your data inside the app.
  • Correct inaccurate data by editing it in the app.
  • Delete individual records in the app, or request deletion of all server data.
  • Withdraw consent for Cloud Backup at any time by signing out.
  • Reset your Advertising ID in your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads.

8. Permissions Used

  • Camera: to capture service photos (stored locally; uploaded only if Cloud Backup is enabled).
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): to optionally import customer contact info from your device address book.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): to remind you of upcoming appointments.
  • Exact Alarms: to schedule precise booking reminders.
  • Internet: for ads, analytics, OAuth login, and Cloud Backup.

9. Children's Privacy

Nailva is intended for nail salon owners (business users) and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via in-app notice or app update. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
